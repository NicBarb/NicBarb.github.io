Complex Recipe's: Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwhich

This recipe will show you how to create the best PBJ sandwhich you'd ever have. It combines the most complex ingredients that would unexpectantly combine so well. Made with things including Peanut Butter, the best jelly, with unicorn tears and love. If you don't like it, you don't have a high enough IQ to understand the delicious flavor.

This sandwhich is so good, it is banned from the World Sandwhich Championships

Recipe Ingredients and How-To

This is what you need:

  • Peanut butter
  • Strawberry Jelly
  • White Bread
  • 2 oz. Unicorn tears
  • Love straight from the heart

Steps:

  1. Get your bread slices
  2. Spread on the peanut butter on both slices
  3. Put jelly on bread
  4. Sprinkle the Unicorn Tears on
  5. Throw your love at it
  6. Put bread slices together
  7. Toss the sandwich into the trash and order some pizza
  8. Wait Approximately 15 minutes and your PBJ will arrive in the form of pizza!
